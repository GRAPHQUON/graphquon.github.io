About the event

GRAPHQON is the annual Quebec-Ontario pre-SIGGRAPH workshop, organized by and for the major East-Canadian computer graphics labs. It is an opportunity for students to present their prospective submissions to a friendly, constructively critical audience and to get valuable feedback.
